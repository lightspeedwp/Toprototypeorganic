4. TEXTURE & MATERIAL INTENSITY
How much texture should we introduce?

Level 1: Micro-Texture Only

Subtle paper grain overlay (5-10% opacity)
Applied to hero sections only
Almost imperceptible, adds depth without weight
Recommended for: Modern organic, fast loading

Level 2: Light Material Variation

Subtle grain + occasional linen/paper texture on cards
Background variations (cream vs. off-white)
Soft, tactile but not heavy
Recommended for: Balanced organic feel

Level 3: Rich Texture (Use Cautiously)

Visible grain, linen, occasional photo textures
Botanical overlays in hero sections
More pronounced material feel
Risk: Can slow performance, increase visual weight

Which texture level (1, 2, or 3)?

ANSWER: Could we blend 1, 2 and 3 depending on the section?


5. SHAPE LANGUAGE & ORGANIC FORMS
How "organic" should shapes be?

Option A: Softened Modern (Subtle)

Increase border-radius slightly (4px → 8px, 6px → 12px)
Keep rectangular cards but with rounded corners
Minimal asymmetry
Best for: Modern organic (refined)

Option B: Pebble/Blob Accents (Moderate)

Standard UI elements remain rectangular
Hero backgrounds, featured cards, CTAs get organic "blob" shapes
Asymmetrical shapes used intentionally, not everywhere
Best for: Balanced organic feel

Option C: Flowing & Asymmetrical (Bold)

Many elements get organic shapes
Wavy section dividers, irregular card shapes
Strong nature-inspired forms
Risk: Can reduce usability if overused

Which shape approach (A, B, or C)?

ANSWER: Moderate and bold with soft borders, radii and shadows.

7. DARK MODE STRATEGY
How should dark mode work with organic design?

Option A: True Dark Organic

Dark backgrounds use deep browns/charcoals (not pure black)
Warm dark tones (#2B2520, #3D3631)
Inverted botanical greens become muted sage
Texture reduces in dark mode (lighter grain)

Option B: Warm Night Mode

Very dark warm neutrals (#1F1D1A, #2C2824)
Accent colors stay earthy but slightly desaturated
Feels like twilight safari, not "dark mode"

Which dark mode approach (A or B)? Or keep the current dark mode and only update light mode?
